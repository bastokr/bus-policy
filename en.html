Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

Bus Dispatch Information (hereinafter referred to as the "App") values the protection of users' personal information and complies with relevant laws and regulations.

1. Information Collected

This App does not provide a membership registration feature and does not collect or store users' personal information on any server.

Data entered by the user, such as dispatch schedules, duty logs, and notification settings, is stored exclusively within the user's device.

2. App Permissions Guide

Notification Permission

The notification permission may be used to provide departure time and turnaround time notification features.

Camera Permission

The camera permission may be used to provide the QR code scanning feature.

Captured videos or images are not saved or transmitted to external servers.

3. Third-Party Provision of Personal Information

The App does not provide users' personal information to any third parties.

4. Retention of Personal Information

Data generated within the App is stored only on the user's device, and the developer cannot access this information.

When the App is deleted, the stored data may also be deleted entirely.

5. External Services

Currently, the App does not use any external services that collect personal information.

If advertisements, login services, cloud backups, or analytics services are added in the future, we will notify you through an update to this Privacy Policy.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be modified due to changes in relevant laws, regulations, or service content.

Any changes will be announced through the App or the app store.
